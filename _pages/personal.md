## Undergrad

I grew up in Grand Haven, Michigan and studied Computer Science at Grand Valley State University. I originally planned on going into industry, but a research project at the Applied Computing Institute under Dr. Zachary DeBruine changed that — we worked on matrix factorization and then sparse compression of single-cell data. That project gave me a collaborator and friend in Seth Wolfgang, two great mentors in Drs. DeBruine and Erin Carrier, and a genuine love for research.
